    Play Bronco Bingo

    It’s spring and time to explore campus! Play Bronco Bingo and enter an opportunity drawing to win prizes this semester.

    Take photos, post on your Instagram or Twitter account, and enter the drawing between now and May 11, 2022.

    How to Play:

    1. Explore the campus! Take a photo of each activity. Post on your public Instagram or Twitter account with #BroncoBingo
    2. Complete a bingo line or cover the entire board by May 11 and enter a drawing to win Bronco swag.

    Download Bronco Bingo Card

    How to Submit:

    • Take a photo of each activity and share it on your public Instagram or Twitter account, or share on IG stories and save them as a highlight.
    • No purchase is required to play. (For example, you can take a photo in front of CenterPointe instead of purchasing a meal.)
    • Once you get a bingo line, fill out this form with your contact info and link to your Instagram or Twitter account.
    • Only one submission per person. The Bronco Bingo team will check your photos and randomly select winners. This contest is not endorsed, administered by, or associated with Instagram or Twitter.
