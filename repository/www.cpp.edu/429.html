    BSBA Learning Goals and Objectives

    Assessment Program

    Through participating in curricular and co-curricular learning opportunities, the graduates of College of Business Administration will develop the following competencies:

    Learning Goals

    #1 - Communication Skills

    Students completing the BSBA program will be able to communicate effectively in a business environment.

    Corresponding Objectives:

    • Students will demonstrate oral communication skills by delivering effective presentations.
    • Students will demonstrate written communication skills by producing professional quality business documents.

    #2 - Critical Thinking

    Students completing the BSBA program will be able to use critical thinking to make business decisions.

    Corresponding Objectives:

    • Students will demonstrate the ability to identify key issues and gather relevant data.
    • Students will demonstrate the ability to solve problems using appropriate techniques to support the decision-making process.

    #3 - Business Functional Knowledge and Global Perspective

    Students completing the BSBA program will demonstrate knowledge of business functions an deal with business problems from a global and integrative perspective.

    Corresponding Objectives:

    • Students will demonstrate an understanding of concepts and basic terminology from all business disciplines.
    • Students will demonstrate understanding of how one functional area impacts another and apply an interdisciplinary approach when solving business problems.
    • Students will demonstrate knowledge of the global business environment.

    #4 - Social Responsibility

    Students completing the BSBA program will demonstrate knowledge of diversity, ethical reasoning, and sustainability.

    Corresponding Objectives:

    • Students will demonstrate understanding of cultural and diversity issues.
    • Students will demonstrate understanding of ethical responsibilities.
    • Students will demonstrate knowledge of sustainability principles and practices.

    #5 - Teamwork Skills

    Students completing the BSBA program will work effectively in teams.

    Corresponding Objectives:

    • Students will demonstrate collaboration, contribution, and timeliness in achieving team goals.
