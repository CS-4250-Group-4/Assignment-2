    MBA Program

    The Polytechnic Advantage


    Flexible Curriculum

    Every schedule is different. Find the right fit with curriculum options to complete an MBA in 2 to 4 years. Courses are offered in a hybrid format, minimizing trips to campus.


    Experienced Faculty

    Faculty teaching in the program have held high level positions at companies and several have worked as consultants and entrepreneurs. They bring projects to class that are relevant to business and team based, consistent with the hands-on educational philosophy of the University.


    Student Support

    The MBA program hosts several speakers and interesting events. MBA students are also supported with exceptional advising and access to career services.

    Required pre-courses for non-business majors are available for those looking to transition careers.

    A Few Facts About Pursuing an MBA

    MBA alumni who left positions with salaries around $50,000 to start full-time programs saw an 80% increase in their salaries upon graduation. (Bloomberg)

    According to survey of business school deans, approximately 20% are aware of companies that only recruit from AACSB-accredited business degree programs. (AACSB Poll)

    Only 7% of current MBA holders said they definitely or probably wouldn’t have gone for the degree (GMAC Post-Grad Study)

    One in 10 b-school alumni are entrepreneurs today. (Bureau of Labor Statistics)

    Program Information

    Outstanding Quality, Exceptional Value

    The Master of Business Administration (MBA) program is designed for working professionals eager to accelerate career potential while continuing daytime employment.

    This dynamic program is offered in evenings, weekends, and hybrid formats to accommodate busy schedules. Our MBA curriculum emphasizes teamwork and hands-on learning.
