    Academic Excellence

    The College of Business Administration has a long-standing tradition of recognizing academic excellence. Each semester, the Dean sends letters to full-time students with 3.5 or higher grade point average to acknowledge the achievement.

    Within the CBA, the top academic honor a student can earn is to be named the Julian A. McPhee Scholar. The McPhee Scholar is selected from each graduating class and wears a green gown during Commencement.
